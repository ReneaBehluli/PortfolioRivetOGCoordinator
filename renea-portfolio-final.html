My Approach

How I'll accelerate your impact

Build systems that scale

I'll create streamlined reporting, milestone tracking, and documentation that makes operations smoother—not more complicated. Think: templates that actually save time, processes that teams want to use.

Just like I did when I digitalized the Ministry of Foreign Affairs' internal systems, bringing calm, steady operational excellence to complex government operations.

Make grantees' lives easier

I design from the grantee's perspective. Requirements that make sense. Check-ins that feel supportive. Reporting that doesn't steal time from their mission. Less friction, more impact.

Coming from Kosovo's "capital desert," I know firsthand what helpful support looks like when resources are scarce.

Turn data into proof

I'll help you tell compelling stories with solid data. Not just numbers—narratives that show funders, partners, and the world that youth leadership works.

At TOKA, every report and presentation included stories, images, and testimonials that made the numbers feel real—turning metrics into compelling proof of impact.

Bridge internal & external teams

With my low-ego, collaborative approach, I keep communication clear, proactive, and human. No bottlenecks, no silos—just teams working together smoothly toward shared goals.

At the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, I coordinated 400+ international stakeholders across high-profile conferences—keeping everyone aligned through transparent communication and relationship-building.

Build feedback loops that actually work

I'll ensure learnings from grantees flow back into how we operate. Systems should evolve based on what we hear from the field—not just what looks good on paper.

Managing 30 trainers at TOKA, I created feedback systems with structured follow-up—not just collecting input, but actually implementing changes based on what we learned.

Champion youth leadership philosophy

I treat community-building as a craft. Every system, every interaction, every process I design will embed RIVET's values: trusting young people, removing barriers, and believing they're the experts on their own lives.

This was the foundation of my work at TOKA—building community through cultural programming that honored local leadership.
